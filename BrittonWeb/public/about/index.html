Who I Am & Why I Started BrittonWeb

Hi, I'm Leland Britton

I’m a 17-year-old self-taught web developer based in Sterling, Kansas. I started BrittonWeb because I believe every small business — no matter how new or local — deserves a clean, professional website that actually helps them grow.

I first got into coding out of curiosity and the desire to build things that worked exactly how I imagined them. I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and fell in love with the process of taking a blank page and turning it into something real. That passion quickly turned into a purpose: helping other people turn their business ideas into digital reality.

How I Work

My websites are 100% hand-coded — no templates, no page builders. That means your site is:

  • Fully mobile-friendly
  • Fast and lightweight
  • Search engine optimized (SEO)
  • Tailored to your business and your brand

Whether you're running a gym, barbershop, or local shop, I’ll help you build a site that works hard for your business and looks good doing it.

What You Can Expect

  • Quick turnaround time
